Established and trusted

Launched in 1995, Prospect is the UK’s outstanding monthly current affairs title - the best writers, robust debate and considered reflection on the biggest issues of our time. Politically independent, with no party-political affiliation or agenda.

Home of fine-writing

Edited by former Guardian editor Alan Rusbridger, we publish in print and digital; producing newsletters, podcasts and events. Our journalism spans politics, science, foreign affairs, economics, climate and the environment, philosophy and the arts.

FAQs

How do I access digital content?

Your subscription gives you full access to the Prospect website and app. Prospect is renowned for mastery of the written word, our digital offering also features other content formats—like podcasts—so you can enjoy Prospect in the way that best suits you.

How soon can I access digital content?

If you place your order on this website, you will have instant access. Orders placed via the telephone or post will begin within 24 working hours.

Accessing the website as a subscriber is very straightforward. Once your order is placed you will receive an email containing all the details you need.

What devices can I use?

You can use the Prospect website on any desktop, tablet or mobile device. The app is available via your app store on iOS or Android.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You will receive our round-up newsletter to your inbox automatically every Saturday morning. You can sign up for our other newsletters, like The Insider written by Andrew Adonis, on our website.
